WHO WE ARE

We are a non-profit organization, established in February 2024, with a heartfelt aim of providing support to the needy and marginalized, especially those living in slum areas across Uganda. At Hearts Of Help, we believe in giving back to the community. We're dedicated to spreading smiles and hope, not only in our local community but also in various communities throughout Uganda. With your help, we can continue our mission of making a difference, one smile at a time. Join us in bringing light to the lives of those who need it most and building a brighter future together.
